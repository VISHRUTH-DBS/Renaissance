The Renaissance (14th to 17th Century)

The Renaissance, meaning "rebirth", was a period of revival of classical learning and wisdom. Famous figures include:

  • Leonardo da Vinci
  • Michelangelo
  • Raphael

The Baroque Period (17th to Early 18th Century)

The Baroque period followed the Renaissance, characterized by grandeur, drama, and movement. Famous figures include:

  • Caravaggio
  • Bernini
  • Rembrandt
